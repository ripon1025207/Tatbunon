About তাঁত বুনন-taat bunon

তাঁত বুনন-taat bunon Limited is a progressive fashion and lifestyle company from Bangladesh. ‘তাঁত বুনন-taat bunon’ is an umbrella multi-category store brand with a unique ethnic, artisanal positioning across the categories of fashion, lifestyle and home décor. For almost two decades, তাঁত বুনন-taat bunon is focusing on handcrafted, native and indigenous products from across the world, with it’s own contemporary deshi spin. The company has established a brand built on fine arts, artisanal heritage and tradition, inherited across various product categories. তাঁত বুনন-taat bunon’s brings together over 200 rural artisans and producers making a tight knit community. It’s progressive methods is being scaled and made adoptable like ready made garments factories with fast-fashion lines, making each product comfortable, durable and affordable.
